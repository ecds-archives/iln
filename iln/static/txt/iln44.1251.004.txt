
[The Inman Steam-ship City of New York]

The Inman steam-ship City of New York, from New York, on the 19th ult., struck on Daunt's Rock on entering Queenstown Harbour on Tuesday morning. She is likely to become a total wreck, the rock having penetrated her bottom under the starboard bunker opposite the mainmast. The passengers, specie, mails, and much of the cargo have been saved.
