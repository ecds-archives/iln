
[During the First Eleven Months of 1863]

During the first eleven months of 1863 there arrived emigrants at New York:--From Ireland, 86,691; Germany, 32,801; England, 16,893; Denmark, 1567; Switzerland, 1058; France, 1195; Wales, 1083; Scotland, 1664; Sweden, 1359; Italy, 405; Holland, 379; Belgium, 456; West Indies, 242; Spain, 152; various others, 574; total, 146,519.
