 

[The Queen has approved of Mr. James Q. Howard]
      

The Queen has approved of Mr. James Q. Howard as Consul at St. John, New Brunswick, for the United States of America; of Mr. H. Sonnenkalb as Consul at Point de Galle, Ceylon, for the King of Prussia; of Mr. Randolph Zapp as Consul at Singapore, for the King of Honover; and of Don Pio de Emparanza as Consul, and of Don Narciso Perez Petinto as Vice-Consul at Sierra Leone, for the Queen of Spain.
