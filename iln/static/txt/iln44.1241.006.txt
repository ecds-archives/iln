
[Last Year there were Exported to Europe]

Last year there were exported to Europe from all parts of the United States, except California, 1,705,882 barrels of flour, 16,150,811 bushels of wheat, 7,106,482 bushels of corn, and 383,316 bushels of rye. In 1862 the exports were 2,376,954 barrels of flour, 29,300,084 bushels of wheat, 12,301,205 bushels of corn, and 910,746 bushels of rye.
