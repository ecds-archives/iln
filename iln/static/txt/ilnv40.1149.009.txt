
Imperial Parliament

IMPERIAL PARLIAMENT.HOUSE OF COMMONS.—Thursday
...General Butler—Sir J. Walsh gave notice that, on the order for going into Supply to-morrow, he should inquire whether her Majesty's Government had received official information of the threatened outrages on women, by General Butler, of New Orleans, in the event of their treating any United States' soldier with disrespect?—Mr. Gregory gave notice of a similar question.

America.—Mr. Hopwood gave notice that to-morrow he would ask the Government whether it was their attention to offer mediation for the purpose of putting an end to the war.
