
[An American Journal]

An American journal states that the 600,000 troops in the Federal Army require per month 14,625,0001b. of pork, or 24,375,000lb. of fresh beef; 136,994 barrels of flour: 48,750 bushels of beans, or 1,050,000lb. of rice; 1,950,000lb. of cocoa, 2,892,000lb. of coffee, 195,000 gallons of vinegar, 12,449 bushels of salt, 8,580,000lb. of potatoes, 1,170,000lb. of candles, and 3,120,000lb. of soap.
