LATEST NEWS FROM ABROAD.
The following telegram has been received through Mr. Reuter's office:--
America.--New York, Aug. 1.--On the 30th ult. General Grant blew up a Confederate fort at Petersburg. He then assaulted and carried a line of Confederate intrenchments. An entire regiment was destroyed, together with the fort. The battle was still progressing on Saturday at noon.
